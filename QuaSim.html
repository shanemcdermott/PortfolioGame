QuaSim is an educational game-based simulator that offers students an adaptive course in the principles of Quantum Cryptography.

  • 4 Lesson Modules
  • Adaptive Challenges and Hints
  • Virtual Assistant/Narrator
  • Cooperative and Competitive Play Modes

As the primary developer for QuaSim, I was responsible for the design and implementation of the game architecture of our lesson system and their integration with Unreal Engine 4. I was also responsible for the implementation of any lesson-specific requirements.
